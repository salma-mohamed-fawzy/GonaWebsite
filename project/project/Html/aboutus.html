Who we are?

Welcome to our slice of paradise in the heart of Al Gouna, where the tranquility of Bali meets the beauty of Egypt's Red Sea coast. Nestled along the pristine shores of the Red Sea, our hotel offers a unique and unforgettable experience inspired by the enchanting island of Bali.

We invite you to escape to our Bali-inspired oasis in Al Gouna and experience the beauty and tranquility of this magical destination for yourself. Whether you are seeking a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or a peaceful retreat, we promise to make your stay with us an unforgettable experience.

Our hotel offers a range of luxurious accommodations designed to ensure your comfort and relaxation throughout your stay. Whether you choose a spacious suite overlooking the sparkling waters of the Red Sea or a cozy bungalow nestled amidst the lush greenery of our gardens, you will find every detail thoughtfully curated to enhance your experience.
